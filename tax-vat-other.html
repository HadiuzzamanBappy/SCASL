Tax, VAT, Tax Exemption Certificate and Other Legal Compliance

Why Tax, VAT and Other Legal Compliance?

Compliance is important to avoid legal consequences as well as reduce cost of doing business. Tax compliance is very important for business to reduce the significant Tax burden. Tax compliance is reducing the cost of business. Value Added Tax (VAT) was introduced by the Government to replace Sales Tax.
