Legal Compliance

RJSC(Register Joint Stock Company Bangladesh)

  • Yearly Compliance for Private Company

    1. Conduct a minimum of 4 Board meetings, and an Annual General Meeting (AGM) each financial year

    2. Conduct Statutory meeting after 1 month of commencement

    3. Commencement of business within 180 days

    4. Auditor Appointment within 30 days

    5. Statutory Meeting within 1 month

    6. Registered Address

    7. AGM within 18 months from incorporation

    8. Preparation of Financial Statements within 4 months.

  • Preparation of Documentation of Annual Return

    1. Extract of AGM Minutes

    2. Schedule-X

    3. Form XII (if needed)

    4. Form IX (if needed)

    5. Form XV (if needed)

    6. Audited Financial Statements

    7. Form 23B
